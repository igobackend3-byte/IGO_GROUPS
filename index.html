Who We Are

IGO Groups (India Green Organics / "India Green") is a Chennai-headquartered diversified group operating across agri-inputs, agri-engineering, organic food retail, agri-fintech, land investment, import-export trade, hospitality and precision agritech. Our companies share one mission: making Indian agriculture more profitable, sustainable and technology-driven — for farmers, dealers, investors and consumers alike.
